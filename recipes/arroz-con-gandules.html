Arroz Con Gandules

Shown: Arroz con Gandules

Arroz con Gandules is a Puerto Rican meal consisting of rice, chickpeas, and a variety of seasonings and other flavors from different vegetables. This meal is most commonly served alongside fried plaintains, known as "tostones", as well as a portion of meat of your choosing. This meal is best enjoyed with the coldest beer money can buy.
